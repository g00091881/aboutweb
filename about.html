About AUS Campus Connect

AUS Campus Connect is a simple website designed for the American University of Sharjah community. The website helps students and staff stay updated on campus events and activities. It has two main pages:

1. Homepage

The homepage welcomes users with a navigation bar, the AUS logo, and a title. It features:

  • A button that links to the Event Submission Page.
  • A carousel that shows images of the AUS campus, including a bird's-eye view, night view, and graduation photos.
  • Static cards displaying sample events, such as National Day and Global Day, with images and brief descriptions.

2. Event Submission Page

This page has a form where users can submit their own events by entering:

  • Event Name
  • Date and Time
  • Location
  • Brief Description
  • Organizer's Contact Information
When users submit the form, their event details are saved in the browser using localStorage and an alert confirms submission.

The goal of the website is to create a simple and user-friendly platform for sharing and discovering events within the AUS campus community.
